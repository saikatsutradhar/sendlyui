How it works

Zero-Knowledge Transfers.

Sendly uses military-grade AES encryption right in your browser. We never see your files, and they are instantly deleted the moment they are downloaded.

1. Upload & Encrypt

Select files up to 500MB. Add a password to mathematically lock your file before it ever leaves your device.

2. Get Your Key

We generate a single-use 6-digit key. Your scrambled file waits on our server, completely unreadable to us.

3. Decrypt Locally

The receiver enters the key and your secret password. The file unlocks directly in their browser.
