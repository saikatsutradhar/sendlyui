Terms & Privacy

Last Updated: March 2026

At Sendly, we believe your data is your own. We designed this service to transfer files as quickly and securely as possible, without hoarding your personal information or keeping your files longer than necessary.

Privacy Policy

1. Zero-Account Architecture

We do not require you to create an account, log in, or provide an email address to use Sendly. Because we do not collect personal profiles, we have nothing to sell to advertisers or third-party data brokers.

2. Ephemeral File Storage

Sendly acts solely as a temporary bridge between two devices. Here is how we handle your files:

  • Instant Deletion: The moment a file is successfully downloaded by the receiver, it is permanently and irreversibly deleted from our servers.
  • Time-Out Deletion: If a file is uploaded but not downloaded within 10 minutes, our automated systems scrub it entirely from our servers.

3. Zero-Knowledge Encryption

Sendly offers optional Client-Side AES Encryption. When you choose to set a password, your file is mathematically locked inside your web browser before it is ever uploaded. We have zero knowledge of your file contents, do not store your passwords, and cannot decrypt your files even if requested.

Terms of Service

1. Acceptable Use

Sendly is designed for personal and professional file sharing. By using Sendly, you agree that you will not use our service to transfer:

  • Illegal, pirated, or copyrighted material you do not own the rights to.
  • Malware, viruses, or destructive code.
  • Child exploitation material or illegal pornography.

While we do not actively monitor file contents due to encryption, any reports of abuse will result in the immediate blocking of the offending IP addresses.

2. Service Availability

Sendly is provided on an "as-is" and "as-available" basis. While we strive for 99.9% uptime, we do not guarantee that the service will be uninterrupted or error-free. We are not liable for any lost data or files that fail to transfer. We recommend always keeping a backup of important files.

3. Rate Limiting and File Size

To ensure fair use and server stability, users are permitted to upload files up to a maximum size of 500MB per standard transfer. For Client-Side Encrypted transfers, the limit is 50MB to prevent browser memory exhaustion on mobile devices. We reserve the right to throttle bandwidth or block IP addresses that abuse the service through automated scripting or excessive uploads.
